Alex Penman

Gray Area Immersive Winter 2017

Concept

To immerse the viewer in a self-reflective state of both calmness and chaos through Kinect motion tracking visualizations.

Experience

The installation space will consist of three walls creating an open room. Two white side walls and one main wall with a video projection captured by a Kinect pointed into the room.

The viewer will see a projection of themselves on the main wall in the installation room.

The viewer's body will be animated using a particle system. There will be no defining lines for the body but the particles will stay in form based on different parameters.

If the viewer stands in a relaxed state, the particles will flow slowly and peacefully, staying well within the boundaries of the viewer's body form.

As the viewer moves and the body is stretched, particles will speed up and begin to move outside of the viewer's form, blurring the lines of the body.

Stretch Ideas For Project

Atmospheric Hardware

Two side walls will be draped in white sheets. One arduino on each wall will have custom components under the sheets with servos to move the sheets in a flowing motion.

The speed of the servos will be determined by the same input as the particle system in the projection.

Foil or any other material may be added behind the white sheet, out of view, to add to the noise.
